Engle Eye

A tongs car simulator with google VR

We have solved dizziness, safe, distance perception, which are the problems from existing HIAB's products. Motion sickness occurs due to the difference between body and camera, so we separated the rotation of the camera and the crane boom. And, in order to ensure the safety of the coworker, we brought 3d surrounding sound from outside to cabin. Finally, when grabbing an object, the mini top-view is placed on VR screen so that the position of the grapple could be ensured. We've made a simulator with our solutions using unreal engine so that consumers can experience it through VR.

  • Date: May. 10. 2019. ~ May. 12. 2019.
  • Place: Wonhyo Electronics Company
  • Category: Hackathon
  • Teamate: Hyunwook Ha, Ham Auejin, Minwoo Choi, Gu Lee, Juan Marcelo Portillo
